🎥 Camera Framing Guide

Rule of thumb: Zoom in so there's only a few centimetres of space between the top of the speaker's head and the top of the frame.

This creates a professional, engaged shot that keeps the focus on the speaker without excessive empty space above.

✅ Good framing:

  • Tight headroom (2-5cm gap at top)
  • Speaker fills most of the frame
  • Eyes in upper third of screen
