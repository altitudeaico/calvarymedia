🎵 January Worship Focus

Throughout January, Calvary is intentionally doing worship differently.

The aim is to re-centre worship as something personal and intentional, rather than something that depends on a band, a stage setup, or a particular sound.

During January:

  • The stage will be intentionally minimal / blank
  • The worship team will be taking a break
  • Worship songs will be played via video/audio
  • Lyrics will be displayed on screen
  • One or more weeks may include a cappella worship

The heart behind this is to remind the church that worship is between the individual and God, whether there is music, a band, or not.

🎥 Camera Framing Guide

Rule of thumb: Zoom in so there's only a few centimetres of space between the top of the speaker's head and the top of the frame.

This creates a professional, engaged shot that keeps the focus on the speaker without excessive empty space above.

✅ Good framing:

  • Tight headroom (2-5cm gap at top)
  • Speaker fills most of the frame
  • Eyes in upper third of screen
