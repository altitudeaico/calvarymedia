🎥 Camera Framing Guide

Rule of thumb: Zoom in so there's only a few centimetres of space between the top of the speaker's head and the top of the frame.

This creates a professional, engaged shot that keeps the focus on the speaker without excessive empty space above.

✅ Good framing:

  • Tight headroom (2-5cm gap at top)
  • Speaker fills most of the frame
  • Eyes in upper third of screen

🔧 Generate Lyrics On The Fly

If you ever need to create lyric slides for a song that isn't pre-prepared, use Calvary's Song Lyric Formatter tool.

📝 How to Generate Lyric Slides:

  1. Go to calvaryhephzibah.co.uk/songlyricformatter
  2. Enter the song title in the title field
  3. Enter the lyrics in the lyrics section, following these rules:
    • Write two lines at a time per slide
    • Keep each line around 25-30 characters (e.g. "He's my rock, He's my fortress")
    • Add a blank line between each pair to separate slides
  4. Click "Generate Slides"
  5. Download the ZIP file
  6. Extract and upload to OBS

📝 Example Format:

He's my rock, He's my fortress
He's my deliverer, in Him will I trust

Praise the Lord, praise the Lord
Let the earth hear His voice

↑ Each pair of lines becomes one slide. The blank line creates the separation.
